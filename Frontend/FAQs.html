Frequently Asked Questions

To sign up for a class, simply browse the available courses and select one. After clicking on the course, create an account or log in. Then, click "Enroll Now" and enter payment details if required. You’ll receive a confirmation email to get started. Happy learning!

We accept a variety of payment methods to make your experience as convenient as possible. Currently, we accept major credit and debit cards, UPI payments. All transactions are processed securely to ensure your payment information is kept safe. If you have any questions or issues with payment, feel free to contact our support team!

Yes, you can cancel your registration! If you need to cancel your enrollment in a class, you can do so before the class starts. Simply go to your account’s “Classes Enrolled” section, find the class you’d like to cancel, and click the “Cancel Enrollment” button. Please note that some classes may have specific cancellation policies or fees, especially if they are paid courses. Be sure to check the class details for any specific terms. If you need assistance with the cancellation process or have any questions, feel free to reach out to our support team!

We value your feedback and encourage you to share your thoughts with us! After completing a class, you can leave feedback by visiting the course page and clicking on the “Leave a Review” button. You’ll be able to rate the class and provide a written review based on your experience. Additionally, if you have any suggestions or concerns that you’d like to share with our team directly, feel free to reach out to us through the "Contact Us" section of the platform. Your input helps us improve and deliver the best learning experience possible.

If you’ve forgotten your password or need to reset it, simply click on the “Forgot Password” link on the login page. You’ll be prompted to enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Follow the instructions in the email to set a new password and regain access to your account.

Many of our courses offer downloadable materials, such as PDFs, worksheets, and slides. Check the course page to see if this option is available. For video content, you can stream them anytime you have an internet connection. Unfortunately, offline video.
