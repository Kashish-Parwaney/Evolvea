Careers at Evolvea

Join us in our mission to empower individuals through learning and sharing knowledge.

Trainers
Role Overview

As a Trainer, you will create and deliver engaging courses that inspire learners.

Responsibilities:
  • Develop course content and materials.
  • Conduct live sessions and record video lessons.
  • Engage with learners through Q&A and feedback.
  • Continuously improve courses based on learner feedback.
Required Skills:
  • Expertise in your subject area.
  • Strong communication and presentation skills.
  • Experience with online teaching platforms.
  • Passion for teaching and mentoring.
Benefits:
  • Flexible schedule.
  • Access to a community of fellow educators.
  • Opportunities for professional development.

Learners
Role Overview

As a Learner, you will have access to a wide range of courses taught by industry experts.Enhance your skills and knowledge in various fields.

Responsibilities:
  • Enroll in courses that interest you.
  • Participate in discussions and complete assignments.
  • Provide feedback on courses to help improve the platform.
Required Skills:
  • A desire to learn and grow.
  • Basic computer skills.
  • Commitment to completing courses.
  • Ability to manage time effectively
Benefits:
  • 90% of Our courses are free of cost!
  • Access to a diverse library of courses.
  • Networking opportunities with trainers and peers.
  • Certificates of completion for your achievements.

Administrators
Role Overview

As an Administrator, you will manage the platform, ensuring a smooth experience for both trainers and learners.

Responsibilities:
  • Oversee course approvals and quality assurance.
  • Manage user accounts and support inquiries.
  • Analyze platform performance and user feedback.
Required Skills:
  • Strong organizational and multitasking abilities.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills.
  • Experience in customer service or community management.
Benefits:
  • Opportunity to shape the future of online learning.
  • Work in a dynamic and innovative environment.
  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
